Extra Information

Will Wood has done many interesting things in addition to his music career. For one, he participates in stand-up comedy and improv, which is very entertaining for many. He also designed the soundtrack for a fictional podcast "Camp Here & There".

Latest Live Album

Will Wood's latest and possibly final album is In Case I Die. It consists many of Will Wood's songs from many albums, in addition to two new songs. All of them are performed solo and are very calm, even the originally chaotic ones.
